Alarming Hills

Everyday, you stream yourself online. Sadly, you barely make much online. But you are filled with excitement when a research organization sponsor you for $10,000. The only setback is that you must stream yourself camping in the park for 10 days. However, you soon realize that something doesn't want you there...

Alarming Hills is a FNAF-inspired horror game created in GDevelop. The game features a mix between the classic camera panning and a new 3D space. You must collect batteries scattered around the campsite, but don't stay out there for too long, otherwise you will lose your sponsorship!

The game contains the following features:

  • 10 nights
  • An additional non-canon hard night
  • 2 endings
  • unique cutscene after every night
  • a tutorial
  • Controller support
  • lore and story
  • Easter Eggs

    • Can You survive?
